Care Guide

How to care for your Wood Style Furniture pieces.

Wood Furniture Care

Your wood furniture is a living material that responds to its environment. With proper care, it will develop a rich patina and last for generations.

Pro Tip: Place your wood furniture away from direct sunlight and heating/cooling vents to prevent cracking and fading.

Cleaning

  • Dust regularly with a soft, lint-free cloth.
  • Clean spills immediately with a dry cloth — never let liquids sit on wood surfaces.
  • Use a slightly damp cloth for stubborn marks, then dry immediately.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals, ammonia-based cleaners, and abrasive pads.
  • Apply a high-quality furniture wax every 6–12 months to maintain the finish.

Upholstery Care

  • Vacuum upholstery weekly using a soft brush attachment.
  • Rotate cushions regularly to ensure even wear.
  • Blot spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth — do not rub.
  • Professional cleaning is recommended every 12–18 months.
  • Avoid placing upholstered pieces in direct sunlight to prevent fading.

Marble & Stone Care

  • Clean with a soft cloth and mild soap and water.
  • Avoid acidic cleaners (vinegar, lemon, citrus) that can etch the surface.
  • Use coasters and trivets to prevent stains and heat damage.
  • Seal marble surfaces annually to protect against staining.

Metal & Brass Care

  • Dust regularly with a soft cloth.
  • Clean with a mild soap and water solution, drying thoroughly.
  • For brass, use a dedicated brass polish to maintain the lustre.
  • Avoid abrasive cleaners that can scratch the finish.

Environmental Recommendations

Maintain a consistent indoor humidity level between 40–60%. Use a humidifier in dry months and a dehumidifier in humid conditions to protect your furniture from warping, cracking, or swelling.
