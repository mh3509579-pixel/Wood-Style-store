Our Warranty

Every piece from Wood Style Furniture is backed by our commitment to exceptional craftsmanship. We stand behind the quality of our materials and the skill of our artisans.

5-Year Limited Warranty — on all wood furniture against manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship.

What Is Covered

Our warranty covers manufacturing defects including:

  • Structural integrity issues (joints, frames, bases)
  • Finish defects in materials and workmanship
  • Hardware failure under normal use
  • Warping or splitting of solid wood components

What Is Not Covered

  • Normal wear and tear, including scratches, dents, and patina development
  • Damage caused by improper use, accidents, or neglect
  • Damage from exposure to extreme temperatures, humidity, or sunlight
  • Fabric and upholstery wear (covered separately — see below)
  • Damage during transit after delivery acceptance

Upholstery Warranty

Upholstery fabrics and leathers are covered for 2 years against manufacturing defects such as seam failure and zipper defects. Natural characteristics of leather — including wrinkles, grain variation, and creasing — are not considered defects.

Making a Warranty Claim

To make a warranty claim, please contact us with your order number, a description of the issue, and photographs. We will respond within 2–3 business days to assess the claim and arrange repair, replacement, or refund as appropriate.

Repair Service

Even after the warranty period, we offer paid repair and restoration services for all Wood Style Furniture pieces. Our artisans can refinish surfaces, repair joints, and reupholster furniture to extend its life for generations.
