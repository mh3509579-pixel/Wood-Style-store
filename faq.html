Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ordering, delivery, and more.

How do I place an order?
Browse our collection, select your desired pieces, and add them to your cart. Proceed to checkout where you can enter your shipping details and payment information. You will receive an order confirmation via email.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, JazzCash, and EasyPaisa.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery within major cities takes 5–7 business days. Remote areas may require 7–14 business days. Handcrafted and made-to-order pieces may take 4–6 weeks. You will receive tracking information once your order ships.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we offer international shipping on a quote basis. Please contact us with your desired items and shipping address for a customized shipping quote.
What is your return policy?
You may return most items within 4 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be in original condition. Custom and made-to-order pieces are final sale. See our Shipping & Returns page for full details.
How do I care for my wood furniture?
Dust regularly with a soft cloth, clean spills immediately, and avoid direct sunlight and extreme humidity. Apply furniture wax every 6–12 months. For detailed instructions, visit our Care Guide.
Do you offer a warranty?
Yes, all wood furniture comes with a 5-year limited warranty against manufacturing defects. Upholstery is covered for 2 years. See our Warranty page for full terms.
Can I request a custom piece?
Absolutely. We offer custom sizing, finishes, and fabric selections for many of our pieces. Contact us with your requirements and our design team will work with you to create a bespoke piece.
How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you will receive an email with tracking information. You can also contact us anytime for order status updates.
